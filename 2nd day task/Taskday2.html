India Wins the ICC T20 World Cup


New Delhi: India has proudly won the ICC T20 World Cup. The match was exciting and fans across the country celebrated the victory with great happiness. The players showed excellent teamwork and confidence throughout the tournament.



After the final match, the captain thanked the fans for their support. He said, "This victory belongs to the whole nation."
Millions of cricket lovers celebrated the moment and shared their joy everywhere. This win is a proud moment for Indian cricket.
